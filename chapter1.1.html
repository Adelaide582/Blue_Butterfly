Mother

Armed with both groceries and a satisfied appetite from the grocery store, you stepped out into the rainy day. The soft pitter-patter of raindrops on your umbrella accompanied your thoughts as you navigated the wet sidewalks. The gray clouds above mirrored the complexity of your unfinished novel.

Lost in your musings, you absentmindedly reached a pedestrian crossing. The traffic light had turned green, signaling the cars to move, but you remained oblivious, preoccupied with the narrative unfolding in your mind.
